Day in the Life of a Cat

Comics: Day in the Life of a Cat

Credits: Tinh Ngo, Jheel Thanki, Redha Al Hammad, Avinash Gyawaliali

Step into the world of a campus cat as it navigates the streets in search of sustenance, offering a glimpse into the daily adventures of feline life. Through an interactive storytelling experience, users will embark on a journey from the cat's perspective, guided by immersive elements like sound and dynamic website scrolling. Join the cat on its quest for food, experiencing the thrill of the hunt and the satisfaction of a well-earned meal through the eyes of our furry protagonist.
